Frequently Asked Questions

What is Fresh Market To GO?

Grocery shopping just got easier with Fresh Market To GO. Fresh Market To GO is our online grocery service powered by Rosie; it's everything you love about Fresh Market, online! Shop for your favorite items from the comfort of anywhere. It's easy, fast and affordable. Simply login, shop for your items, and select a pickup time and arrive at the store to pick them up.

Where is Fresh Market To GO available?

Fresh Market To Go is only available at our Jeremy Ranch location. However, we have teamed up with our sister stores Macey’s and Dick’s Market to offer convenient pickup locations at many of our Fresh Market stores. Shop Macey’s Anywhere for convenient curbside pickup at the following Fresh Market locations: East Ogden, South Ogden, American Fork, Provo, 39th South, 17th South and 21st South. Shop Dick’s Market Online for convenient curbside pickup at our East Layton Fresh Market store.

How much does it cost?

The convenience fee for all pickup orders is $1.99. Prices online may vary from those in-store. All advertised discounts and promotions found in the Fresh Market weekly ad are honored at the same price you would receive in the store. If you notice an item that you believe is priced incorrectly, please contact us here

Can I pay with a gift card?

We do not accept gift cards at this time. Payment must be made by credit or debit card at time of purchase.

What if I miss my pickup or delivery window?

If you miss your timeslot, we will contact you to reschedule. If you know ahead of time you will not be able to meet your pre-selected time, please contact us to reschedule.
